LOURDES REYES

I am a Freelance Make-Up Artist. Since I started my career, it was impossible to stop. This is my passion. From an early age, I was fascinated by the industry. I further enhanced my training by working in school events doing my peers' make-up. Although I have worked with many people around New Hampshire. I am currently seeking to gain more experience and work with people from different states. I feel that, as an artist, it is crucial to constantly refresh my ideas and I find inspiration everywhere I look. I love the diverse creative people that I meet continuously with my work. They have all inspired me in one way or the other. As a young child, I was always fascinated by the world of fashion & beauty, while my peers were busy playing with toys I was more likely to be found with my head in glossy magazines reading about famous designers, models, and make-up artists. As the years went by, my love for the fashion and beauty industry grew deeper. My approach to style is simple: I'm all about using the right products in the right places to highlight a person's best features and natural beauty.
